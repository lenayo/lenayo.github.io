As you "stroll" through this online tour, you can read all about the winning hospital's design and construction, download its floor plan, shop for design titles in an online bookstore, and even enter next year's design competition. You can also find out more information on featured products in the reception area, ICU, surgical suite, and other areas of the hospital.

To navigate the virtual tour, simply click on the picture to the right. Then, using your mouse, you can "spin" 360 degrees around the room; zoom in or out of the picture; and, by clicking on a doorway, move into another room. (Hint: Once you begin the tour, a red dot on the hospital floor plan will indicate your position. Click on any of the blue dots to go to another room.) The tour uses QuickTime VR technology. To download this free software, please click on the QuickTime logo to the right.
