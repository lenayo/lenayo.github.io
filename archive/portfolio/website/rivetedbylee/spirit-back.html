With a basic tee shirt and a great-looking pair of jeans you'll be well on your way...

Try pairing a fitted tee with a flowing printed skirt and strappy sandals. Or for this season's hot black and white looks, you can't go wrong with a basic white or black tee and a pencil skirt.

Jeans are the perfect match for your basic tee alone or with a cute cardigan. If it's a more buttoned-up look you're going for, pair your jeans with an oxford shirt and blazer. And if hitting the town is more your style, low-rise jeans and a tiny tank top or glittery top are a great alternative.
